About Me

I've been interested in technology for as long as I can remember, starting my journey as a programmer from the age of 12 when I picked up HTML and CSS. To this day I continue to search for more cool stuff to learn, both in my studies and on my own or with mates.

Currently I study Computer Science with a major in Software Development at Swinburne full time, undertaking a full time internship at OnQ Software as a software developer as a part of my studies.

Full Stack

I have professional experience developing full stack applications using Angular, Microsoft SQL Server, ElasticSearch, JavaScript/TypeScript, and C#. I've also had experience at hackathons and in personal projects working with PHP, Flask/Flast-RESTful, and React.

I currently work as a software developer for OnQ Software, developing the new QLIMS9 Laboratory Information Management Software platform. Day to day I fix workflow bugs, create/implement user facing management tools for UI/UX, and design/implement the UI/UX of our platform.

Game Dev

I've been a hobbyist game developer for going on 4 years now, operating both on my own and as a part of a team. I've experimented with various engines, including Unreal Engine 4, Godot, and Unity. Of these engines I primarily use and know the most about Unity.
